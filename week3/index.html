How to apply to Vocational Qualification in Business

  1. 1. Online application and supporting documents

    Before submitting your application, see the

  2. Kindly prepare all supporting documents beforehand and include them in your online application. You may also submit paper copies of the supporting documents. Please note that applications submitted without the supporting documents, including upper secondary school certificates and an English language certificate, will not be considered.

  1. 2. Admission Process

  2. Applications will be considered after the application period. The applicants’ eligibility will be evaluated based on the online application and supporting documents.
    Selected applicants will be invited to submit a video application based on their written application and supporting documents, including educational and English language certificates as well as residence permit.
    Please note that you will be required to prove your English language skills by submitting a language certificate with your application. Please see the Applicants’ Guide for detailed information..

  1. 3. Confirming the study place

  2. A list of admitted students who have given a permission to publish their name on our webpages will be published on our webpage in the news section.
    Confirm your study place. All admitted students will receive an admission letter by mail. Detailed instructions for confirming your study place will be sent with the letter.
